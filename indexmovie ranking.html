Best Movies of All Time by Vivian

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Saving Private Ryan

This movie is an all-time clasic war movie. It is based on World War 2.
It has an excellent battle sequence of the Battle of Normandy in the beginning.
The ending was breath-taking.

The Ghost and Mrs. Muir

This movie is a classic movie from 1947.
Its basically about a widow who moves into a seaside cabin.
It is haunted by a sea captian.
He help's her to write a book about his life. In which he falls in love with her.

Avengers: Age of Ultron

Okay, okay, I know its a Marvel super hero movie.
This movie was the beginning of my love affair with super hero movies.
Tony Stark devices a plan to bring world peace using a septor and Artificial Intelligence.
He creates a robotic being that decides to destroy humanity instead of saving it.
It has humor, drama, and action in it.
